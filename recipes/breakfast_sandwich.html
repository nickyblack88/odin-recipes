The Perfect Breakfast Sandwich

Description

Breakfast sandwiches are the best way to combine bacon and eggs together in one beautiful bite!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Bacon
  • Bread
  • Ketchup to taste...

Steps

  1. Fry bacon to your prefered crispiness, then fry eggs to over easy.

  2. Toast bread while frying and then put butter on one side
  3. Combine evrything together with a splash of kethcup if you desire and EAT
